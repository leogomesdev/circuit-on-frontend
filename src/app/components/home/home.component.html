Welcome

info  On your TV, you can add the /view address to your favorites and set it as your home page

info  The View page will automatically update the image based on the current schedule. You do not need to refresh the TV browser, only open it once and the magic will happen

info  It is not required to schedule the same image for 5:50 am and 5:35 pm. If there is no other picture to show in this meantime, at 5:35 pm it will still still show the last picture on the system

Instructions:

  1. Use the menu on the left to upload your Images and manage your Schedules
  2. Use the Current Schedule menu to preview your schedule
  3. On your TV, open the internet browser, and access the view page
